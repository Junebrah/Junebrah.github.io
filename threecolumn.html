Donald Trump is a billionaire real estate mogul and television personality. He was first born in June 14, 1946, in Queens, New York. In 1971 he became involved in large, profitable building projects in Manhattan. In 1980, he opened the Grand Hyatt, which made him the city's best known and most controversial developer. In 2004 Trump began starring in the hit NBC reality series The Apprentice. He currently have five children.

Trump had start running for president since 3 months ago saying that he will make America great again. He is known to be very rich and currently has 25% in the polls and is currently leading the presedential elections.
